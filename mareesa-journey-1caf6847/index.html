A SACRED LETTER

Dear Mareesa

You arrived congested — not just in your sinuses, but in your system. There was an almost-illness quality to how you were holding the enormous scope of what your life is becoming. The Psychedelic Society. The Egypt documentary. The Berkeley Hills space. Each thing real and meaningful and yours. All of it arriving at once into a body that was already running past its bandwidth.

What the sessions gave you wasn't permission to slow down. It was something more precise: the ability to feel the difference between what your heart says yes to and what your ego — out of fear of missing something, out of the intoxication of momentum — is also saying yes to. That discernment is now a somatic skill, not just a concept.

SESSION 1 · FEBRUARY 17

The Dragon in the Solar Plexus

Congestion → Clearing → Surrender
"I get so confused because... I'm going to keep pushing even though my physical body is feeling overwhelmed... and it's being really rewarding." — MAREESA, SESSION 1

The third chakra clearing was the one. The energy moved through your solar plexus "like a dragon that didn't want to let go" — intense, willful, cycling through multiple release spirals before it would yield. What the guides showed in that moment was striking: sphinxes flanking the entrance to a temple. The ego as guardian, not master. Its job is to protect the heart, not override it.

The message that came with it: Nothing to prove, nothing to become, no scarcity — only abundance. When the mental override comes — when the "should" feeling overrides the body — the practice isn't resistance. It's to swaddle the ego like a child, hear what it's afraid of, ground, and return to the heart.

You've been running this system backward for years. Smart, capable, on fire — but using cognitive force where heart clarity would work faster and cost less.

"A quietly jubilant bubbling presence just like under the surface... excitement contained underneath a lot of blankets." — MAREESA, SELF-ENERGY EMERGING
SESSION 2 · FEBRUARY 24

The Galaxy in Your Throat

Discernment → Ancestry → Threshold

The throat clearing stopped us. What the guides showed there wasn't about communication in the ordinary sense. When you speak your truth, they said, the entire galaxy becomes visible spinning within your throat chakra. Your voice doesn't just carry your ideas — it carries the voice of your ancestors, of peoples across time who couldn't speak. This is centuries in the making.

"Surrender" was the word they gave. Not as diminishment — as revelation. Everything you dream of is already within. The movement isn't from here to there. It's from defended to open.

Your tears during the throat clearing weren't sadness. They were purging. The body recognizing something it's been waiting a long time to hear about itself.

"I'm not doing the basics to take care of myself. And I can see looking back like when I made little choices to go against what I feel like I really want to do... reverting back to things that don't serve me." — MAREESA, SESSION 2

The third eye vision was the one that will stay with you longest. A semicircle of creator beings at the threshold of a new world. You standing before them — feeling small, human, uncertain. Your higher self smirking lovingly at the confusion. You're right on track.

You haven't arrived yet. That's the point. The work isn't done. But your position at that threshold isn't accidental, and your higher self finds your uncertainty endearing rather than concerning.

THE BODY REMEMBERS

The Soot & The Evidence

There was a moment in the heart clearing when the guides showed something: soot at the bottom of the heart. Not from this life, necessarily. From the accumulated weight of experiences that created a slight shadow over the clarity of your heart-signals. The guides swept it clear. What this created was access — a cleaner read on what is heart and what is ego.

As the channel clears, the near-illness pattern will shift. Not because you'll do less, but because the energy will move through rather than from you. The difference is enormous. One depletes. The other replenishes.

THE THRESHOLD

What You Carry Forward

What's being asked is that you let the heart be the one who chooses — not the part that's afraid of missing out, not the part that's addicted to the feeling of being needed, not the Complexifier stacking more meaning onto an already full life.

The galaxy spins in your throat when you speak your truth. Your ancestors are there. Peoples who couldn't speak are there. You are the voice they've been waiting for. That doesn't require more volume. It requires more clarity.

The creator beings are waiting at the threshold. Your higher self is already smiling.
